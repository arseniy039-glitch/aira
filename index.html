FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can Aira be set up?
Most clients are fully live within 48 hours of the onboarding call. We handle all the technical setup — you just tell us how your business works.
What languages does Aira speak?
Aira communicates fluently in English, Portuguese, Russian, German, Spanish, and French. Additional languages are available on request.
Can I customise Aira's name and personality?
Yes — on the Professional plan, Aira gets a custom name, voice, and communication style tailored to your brand. Your clients will never know it's AI unless you tell them.
What happens if Aira can't handle something?
Aira knows its limits. Anything outside its configured scope gets flagged to you immediately with full context, so you can step in within seconds.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No long-term contract. Monthly subscription, cancel anytime. The setup fee covers your onboarding and configuration.
Do you offer a partner or reseller programme?
Yes — we work with recruitment agencies, business consultants, and executive coaches who want to offer Aira to their clients. Contact us to discuss partnership terms.
